Save time and space with sparse molecular fingerprints

See the notebook

With the recent explosion in the number of purchasable molecules, it's become increasingly important to handle large molecule datasets efficiently in both disk space and time. One way to do this is the use of sparse matrices. Since I use python, and scipy has a great module for 2D-sparse matrices, I now process all molecular fingerprints into scipy.sparse.csr_matrix.
